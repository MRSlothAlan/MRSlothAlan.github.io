Cutiepie

My first computer vision Arduino project

Build a simple yet cute robot that works!

By Alan Poon, 29/4/2019

I recalled that I have bought an Arduino UNO and a grove starter kit on the spur of the moment after visiting a tech exhibition last year. At that time, I have no idea about how to use them at all.

About three months ago, I was required to create an interactive prototype in a GE (gateway education) course. So, I decided to make good use of the expensive hardwares that I have bought...

In the documentation, I have decribed how I made this simple DIY robot. It is really a lot of fun for me.
